Physical Health

◎ Did you know that: Urban residents tend to be more physically active than others. This can be due to the fact that their lifestyles generally include more walking from place to place.

◎ Urban residents have access to better healthcare and fitness centers. The ease of these facilities' access can promote physical well-being.

Mental Health

◎ To some extent, mental health can be as important as physical health. Luckily, the city can help with that.

◎ Urban Green Spaces are spaces/sections within cities that are reserved with nature; things like grass, trees, etc. This can be a great way to destress.
